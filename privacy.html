Focus On Yourself

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Short version: Focus On Yourself does not collect, share, or sell your personal data. Everything you enter stays on your device and your private iCloud account. We have no servers, no analytics, and no third-party tracking.

1. What Data We Collect

Focus On Yourself stores the following data locally on your device and in your personal iCloud account:

  • Your first name (optional, entered during onboarding)
  • Your preferred affirmation categories
  • Daily check-in records (mood rating and optional journal notes)
  • Streak history and check-in dates
  • App preferences and settings (e.g. reminder time, notification preferences)

This data is stored using Apple's NSUbiquitousKeyValueStore (iCloud Key-Value Store) and CloudKit with your personal iCloud account. It is never transmitted to our servers because we have none.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Display your name in the app's greeting
  • Show your check-in history, streaks, and calendar
  • Personalize affirmations to your selected categories
  • Sync your data across your own Apple devices via iCloud

3. iCloud Sync

If you are signed in to iCloud, your app data syncs privately between your own Apple devices through Apple's CloudKit infrastructure. This sync is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. We cannot see, access, or modify your iCloud data.

4. No Third-Party Sharing

We do not share your data with any third party — ever. We use no advertising SDKs, no analytics frameworks, no crash reporting services, and no social integrations.

5. Local Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, Focus On Yourself schedules local notifications on your device. These notifications are generated entirely on-device and involve no external servers or network requests.

6. Apple Intelligence (AI Affirmations)

On supported devices (iPhone 15 Pro or newer running iOS 26 or later), Focus On Yourself Pro can generate personalized affirmations using Apple Intelligence. All AI processing occurs entirely on-device via Apple's on-device models. No data is sent to any server — not ours, not Apple's AI servers — as part of this feature.

7. Subscriptions

Focus On Yourself Pro subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see your payment information. Subscription management is handled by Apple at apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions.

8. Children's Privacy

Focus On Yourself is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since no data is transmitted to us at all, there is no risk of inadvertent collection from users of any age.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above and post the new version at this same URL. We will not reduce your privacy rights without clear notice in the app.
