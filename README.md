## About

**Focus On Yourself** is your daily companion for building a positive mindset — one affirmation, one check-in, one moment of intention at a time.

- Daily affirmations across 6 curated categories
- Mood check-ins with optional journal notes
- Streak tracking and calendar history
- AI affirmations powered by Apple Intelligence (Pro)
- No account required — data stays on your device and syncs via iCloud
